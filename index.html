Frequently Asked Questions

What is the top speed of the 2025 YZF-R1M?
The 2025 YZF-R1M can reach a top speed of around 299 km/h (186 mph) under optimal riding conditions.
Does the MT-10 come with quick shifter support?
Yes, the 2025 Yamaha MT-10 includes a factory-installed quick shifter for seamless gear transitions.
What kind of warranty do Yamaha bikes come with?
Yamaha typically offers a 2-year standard warranty, which may vary depending on the region and model.
Are Yamaha bikes beginner-friendly?
Yes, models like the Yamaha R3 and MT-03 are designed with lightweight frames and smooth power delivery, making them ideal for new riders.
How often should I service my Yamaha motorcycle?
It’s recommended to service your bike every 3,000–5,000 km or as stated in the owner’s manual for optimal performance and safety.
